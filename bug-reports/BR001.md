# BR001 — Download Button Does Not Redirect to App Store

**Date:** 2026-05-23
**Tested by:** Greice Santellano
**Severity:** Medium
**Status:** Open

---

## Environment
- Device: (seu celular — ex: iPhone 13)
- OS: (ex: iOS 18)
- Browser: (ex: Safari / Chrome)
- URL tested: (cola o link do site do Habibis)

---

## Pre-condition
User is on the Habibis website homepage, 
not logged in.

---

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Access the Habibis website
2. Locate the "Download the app" button 
   in the middle section of the page
3. Tap the button

---

## Expected Result
User is redirected to the App Store 
or Google Play to download the app.

## Actual Result
Page scrolls back to the top instead 
of redirecting to the download page.

---

## Additional Finding
The download button at the bottom 
of the page works correctly. 
Only the middle button is affected.
